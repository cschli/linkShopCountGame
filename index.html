Link's Nightmare


Link (you) has been transported to an alternate dimension! You have been tasked with the monumentous role of providing your paying customers with the correct change in rupees or you will be savagely attacked (it's a harsh world).

There are 4 types of rupees below. Click on each with give you a certain number of rupees, adding them to your rupee change count.

You must match your change amount with target change amount to satisfy your customer. If you go over, you lose a heart...from being beat up for your poor service.

To make this extra fun (although not for Link), the value of each rupee is hidden at the beginning of each round, so you must click on them first to know what they're worth.

Each time a round starts (new target value), the rupee amounts are randomized!

Every customer you satisfy adds to your win count (and your customer trophy collection!)

If you lose all 4 lives, it is game over and you must start over (click Restart button).
